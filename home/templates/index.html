Who are we?

We love running. We run in the city all week and then race around the Great British Countryside all weekend. We've always struggled to find stock of the fastest running shoes in one place. Running shops they always have a limited choice of racing shoes and online the choice is overwhelming but there is never enough detail about what they are like to run in to make an informed decision. We're here to change that. We take the leading models of a particular type of shoe and go and run around in them... a lot. We then report back on the best aspects of each shoe to help you make the best investment.

Our pricing fairly reflects the cost of selling the shoe to you. We're a small family run business and we don't have any economies of scale. We buy all the shoes we test and seek to highlight their best features so you can make an informed decision.
