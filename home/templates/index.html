Who are we?

I love running. i run to work in central London throughout the week and then race around the Great British Countryside all weekend. I've always struggled to find stock of the fastest running shoes in one place. Whenever I go into a running shop they always have very limited choice of racing shoes and whenever I look online the choice is overwhelming but there is never enough detail about the shoe and what they are like to run in for me to make an informed decision. I set up this shop to change that. We take the leading models of a particular type of shoe and go and run around in them... a lot. We then report back on the best aspects of each shoe to help you make the best investment and race the fastest. Our pricing fairly reflects the cost of selling the shoe to you. We're a small family run business and we do't have any economies of scale. We buy all the shoes we test and seek to highlight there best features so you can make an informed decision.
